Skills and Proficiencies

learned over four years at the University of Washington and twenty-or-so years in this beautiful world

Information Assurance and Cybersecurity

During the summers of 2016 and 2017, I had acquired practical experience in web penetration test through my internships at Cagetest Inc., a cybersecurity startup based in Shanghai, China, by conducting techniques including but not limited to SQLi, RCE, XSS and Java deserialization.

Coding

Having been studying at the University of Washington for four years, I did projects on Mobile Apps in Swift, database and data structure implementation in Java, machine learning algorithms in Python, RDBMS on MySQL/SQL Server, NoSQL and parallel data processing using Spark, and more to come!

User-centered Design

I interned at Kaikai Kiki New York's Seattle office as a data and project management intern. I conducted an ethnographic research on the original file storage structure, re-organized the system and ultimately streamlined the workflow for retrieving documents as well as adding new ones into the archive.
